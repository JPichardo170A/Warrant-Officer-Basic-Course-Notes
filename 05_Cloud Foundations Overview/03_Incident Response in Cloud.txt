# Incident Response in AWS and Azure:
 
 	Incident Response Steps:
 			Step 1 	Alert triage 			(Quickly assess alerts to determine if actions is needed)
 			Step 2  Incident 			(Classification (Define the type and scope of the incident)
 			Step 3  Initial Containment 		(Stop the attack from spreading)
 			Step 4  Deep analysis 			(LOGS understand Exactly what happened)
 			Step 5  Eradication 			(Remove attacker access and artifacts)
 			Step 6  Recovery 			(restore normal operations securely)
 			Step 7  Reporting & Lessons Learned 	(Improve security posture and IR readiness)
