# 🛡️ Defensive Cyberspace Operations (DCO)

*Module 03 — Defensive Cyberspace Operations | Foundations*

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- Understand the cyberspace actions and layers
- Identify the objectives of Defensive Cyberspace Operations
- Describe the phases of DCO (Screen, Contain, Clear, Secure)
- Understand Operational Preparation of the Environment (OPE)
- Know CPT work roles and organizational structure
- Understand the CPT operations process triggers and planning phases

---

## 🧱 Cyberspace Actions

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Cyberspace Attack | Offensive operations in cyberspace |
| Cyberspace Defense | Defensive operations to protect networks |
| Cyberspace ISR | Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance |
| OPE | Operational Preparation of the Environment |

---

## 🧱 Layers of Cyberspace

| Layer | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Cyber Persona Layer | User identities and accounts |
| Physical Layer | Hardware, cables, infrastructure |
| Logical Layer | Software, protocols, data flows |

---

## 🧱 Objectives of DCO

DCO objectives are unique to a Commander's Mission:

- Deter, Deny, and/or Defeat Adversary OCO
- Gain Time
- Defend Critical Assets
- Control Key Terrain in Cyberspace (KT-C)

---

## 🔍 Intelligence Support for DCO

- Organically assigned intelligence
- J2, G2, A2, and etc.
- Data intel from current mission and leveraged

---

## 📋 Phases of DCO

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Screen** | Initial monitoring and detection |
| **Contain** | Containment decisions to limit adversary impact |
| **Clear** | Remove adversary presence from the network |
| **Secure** | Harden and verify network integrity |

---

## 🧱 Operational Preparation of the Environment (OPE)

### Leadership Support Element
- Team Lead
  - Cyber Operations Planner
  - Operations
  - NCOIC

### Mission Element (ME)
- Mission Element Team Lead
  - Crew Lead
  - Host Analyst
  - Network Analyst

### Operations Support
- Host Analyst
- Analytic Support Officer
- Data Engineer
- Network Technician
- Network Analyst

### Intelligence Support
- All-Source Analyst M/S
- All-Source Analyst S
- All-Source Analyst B

> 🧠 **Tip:** CPT Work Roles are defined in (CTM) 7-0.1 Joint Cyberspace Training and Certification Standards (JCT&CS) and (CTM) 7-0.2 Cyber Mission Force.

---

## 🔧 What Triggers the CPT Operations Process

- Threat Intelligence
- Campaign Plan
- MCA Detection

---

## 📋 Planning the Mission

| Phase | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Phase 1 | Objectives, Effects, and Guidance |
| Phase 2 | Terrain Identification and Prioritization |
| Phase 3 | Capability Analysis and Force Allocation |
| Phase 4 | Orders Production and Dissemination |
| Phase 5 | Tactical Planning and Mission Execution |
| Phase 6 | Assessment and Analysis of MOPs |

---

## ✅ Summary

- DCO encompasses cyberspace defense across persona, physical, and logical layers
- Objectives are mission-specific: deter, deny, defeat adversary OCO
- Four phases: Screen → Contain → Clear → Secure
- CPTs are organized into Leadership, Mission Element, Operations Support, and Intelligence Support
- Operations are triggered by threat intel, campaign plans, or MCA detection
